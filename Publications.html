Submission Guidelines

Authors willing to present their research work  
can submit the abstract electronically (not  
more than 300 words) to ncrtma@gmail.com
before 17/02/2020.

All submitted papers will be reviewed by  
Advisory Committee of the conference and a  
proceeding with all accepted papers will be  
released on the day of the conference.

The scrutinized and accepted papers will be  
published in Scopus indexed journal / UGC  
recognized journal.
